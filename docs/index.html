About Us

Rowdy Media, LLC is a digital media company focused on creating innovative applications that enhance everyday experiences. We combine cutting-edge technology with thoughtful design to build products that people love to use.

Our mission is to empower users through intuitive, powerful digital solutions that solve real-world problems. We believe in the transformative power of technology when it's accessible, beautiful, and genuinely useful.

Our flagship product is Meal Maker 3000, an AI assisted meal prep and recipe app that helps people discover and create amazing meals.

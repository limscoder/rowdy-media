Remembrance Lost

A 2-player D&D 5e campaign

About the Campaign

Remembrance Lost is a character-driven, two-player cooperative campaign designed for D&D 5e. One player takes the role of DM/narrator while simultaneously controlling Augustus, the Oathkeeper paladin, while the other player controls Elara, a druid protagonist. The campaign explores themes of memory, devotion, betrayal, and the bonds that define us.

Elara awakens in Azzurro City with complete amnesia—no memory of her past, her motivations, or even her companion Augustus. Her last conscious moment was an attack by cultists of Malachor, a corrupted archmage who has turned to forbidden Feywild pacts to dominate the natural world. Augustus, bound by his sacred Oath of Devotion to Elara, takes up the burden of guiding her journey, knowing she must restore her memories to prevent catastrophe.

Together, they must travel across the Azure Coast—through pristine white cities, divine shrines, ancient forests, and a corrupted island fortress—to recover the Sanctuary Bloom and place it within the Nexus, a sacred site of ancient druidic power. Their success will not only restore Elara's memories but save the wildlands themselves from crystallization and corruption.

Campaign Features

  • Perfect for 2 Players: Designed specifically for one DM and one player, making it ideal for couples or pairs of friends
  • Beginner Friendly: Configured for beginner characters with optional difficulty scaling
  • Rich Visual Materials: Includes character portraits, location maps, and item illustrations
  • Complete Story Arc: Journey through 8 unique locations from start to finish
  • Flexible Roleplay: The relationship between Augustus and Elara can be romantic, religious, or duty-bound—shaped by player choice

Campaign Locations

The adventure spans eight beautifully detailed locations across the Azure Coast:

  1. Azzurro City - Pristine coastal metropolis where the campaign begins
  2. Glass Shrine - Temple dedicated to Torm, god of duty and loyalty
  3. Whispering Canyon - Dangerous chasm crossed via narrow bridge, guarded by the Riddle-Keeper
  4. Bluebell Wilds - Ancient forest containing the Sanctuary Bloom and corrupted guardians
  5. Forest Path - A connecting route through corrupted woodland, home to Thornbark the Awakened Tree
  6. Obsidian Watch - Malachor's island fortress base of operations
  7. Obsidian Tower - The corrupted mage's ritual chamber within the fortress
  8. The Nexus - A mountaintop sacred site with standing stones and divine power, location of the campaign's climax

Principal Characters

Augustus - Paladin of Torm

Augustus is a paladin of Torm, the Loyal Fury, bound by the sacred Oath of Devotion. Unlike many of his order who dedicate themselves to abstract ideals of justice and law, Augustus's oath took a far more personal form. He swore himself to a single individual, making Elara not merely someone he protects, but his devotion, the living embodiment of everything his sacred vows represent.

Elara - Druid of the Circle of the Land

Elara is a druid who once called the Seaside Manor in Azzurro City her home. Born to a wealthy family of the Azure Coast's elite, she was drawn to the natural world from a young age. She has lost her memory and can remember neither her own past, her motivations, nor the history of her companion Augustus. She does remember her druidic skills.

Malachor the Unbinder

Malachor was once a respected archmage of Azzurro City who became obsessed with unbinding the boundary between the Material Plane and the Feywild. His plan is both elegant and terrible: by unbinding the druidic's divine link to the wildlands and bringing corrupt Feywild magic to the Azure Coast, he will cause the wildland's magic to crystallize, becoming a resource he alone can harvest and command.

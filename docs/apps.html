Honey Garlic Salmon

‚è±Ô∏è 20 minutes üçΩÔ∏è Serves 4

Pan-seared salmon fillets glazed with a sweet and savory honey garlic sauce that caramelizes beautifully.

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets (6 oz each)
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp fresh ginger, grated
  • Sesame seeds and green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Whisk together honey, garlic, soy sauce, and ginger in a small bowl
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat
  3. Season salmon with salt and pepper, place skin-side up in the skillet
  4. Cook for 4-5 minutes, then flip and cook another 3 minutes
  5. Pour honey garlic sauce over salmon and cook for 2 more minutes
  6. Garnish with sesame seeds and green onions before serving

A quick and flavorful stir-fry with aromatic Thai basil, fresh vegetables, and your choice of protein.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken or tofu, sliced
  • 2 cups Thai basil leaves
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 Thai chilies, sliced
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Jasmine rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a wok over high heat
  2. Stir-fry garlic and chilies for 30 seconds
  3. Add protein and cook until nearly done

  5. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, and fish sauce
  6. Toss in Thai basil and stir until wilted
  7. Serve immediately over jasmine rice

Chocolate Lava Cakes

‚è±Ô∏è 25 minutes üçΩÔ∏è Serves 6

Decadent individual chocolate cakes with a molten center that flows out when you cut into them.

Ingredients:

  • 6 oz dark chocolate, chopped
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • Pinch of salt
  • Butter and cocoa powder for ramekins
  • Vanilla ice cream for serving

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425¬∞F and prepare 6 ramekins with butter and cocoa powder
  2. Melt chocolate and butter together until smooth
  3. Whisk eggs, egg yolks, and sugar until thick and pale
  4. Fold chocolate mixture into eggs
  5. Gently fold in flour and salt
  6. Divide batter among ramekins
  7. Bake for 12-14 minutes until edges are set but center jiggles
  8. Let cool for 1 minute, invert onto plates, and serve with ice cream

Vegetable Pad Thai

‚è±Ô∏è 25 minutes üçΩÔ∏è Serves 4

A vegetarian twist on the classic Thai noodle dish with a perfect balance of sweet, sour, and savory flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz rice noodles
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, snap peas)
  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • 3 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup peanuts, crushed
  • Bean sprouts and lime wedges for serving
  • Scallions and cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Soak rice noodles in warm water for 30 minutes, then drain
  2. Mix tamarind paste, soy sauce, and brown sugar for the sauce
  3. Heat oil in a wok and scramble eggs, set aside
  4. Stir-fry vegetables until crisp-tender
  5. Add noodles and sauce, toss until noodles are coated
  6. Add scrambled eggs and toss to combine
  7. Serve topped with peanuts, bean sprouts, scallions, and lime wedges
